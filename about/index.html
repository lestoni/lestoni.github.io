Hello there, i appreciate you stopping by, my name is Tony Mutai or lestoni if you prefer. I live in Nairobi, Kenya.

I am a software engineer primarily specializing in designing and building of RESTFul API’s aka programming for the internets. The love for working with API’s stems from interest and enjoyment of working with web scale distributed and concurrent systems. I mostly code with NodeJS, Golang but i am working towards being a polyglot. I like to engage myself in mad science stuff to learn more, experiment and teach others. In my spare time, i enjoy photography, making music, doodling art, random travel adventures and hanging out with friends and family.
